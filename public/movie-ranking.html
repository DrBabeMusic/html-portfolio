The Best Movies According to Brian

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Donnie Darko

This movie is an excellent depiction of time travel. Donnie also has to come to terms with his life and how it effects others.

Zoolander

A ridiculously stupid movie that is hilarious. This is my favorite Owen Wilson role.

Stepbrothers

Every time this movie is on, I can't help myself and I have to watch it. Comedy gold.
